## üßÝ Dev Notes & Hypotheses
- **Windows case-collision (fixed).** SLP1 is case-significant (`a`‚âÝ`A`) but Windows' FS is
  case-insensitive ‚Üí per-letter shard files `a`/`A` collided & truncated, losing 23k records
  until bucket names were **case-folded to upper**. Any future per-key file sharding must fold.
- **Pages slot is single-per-repo.** This repo exists *because* SanskritLexicography's Pages
  serves the PWG article dashboard (`gh-pages`); don't try to co-host ‚Äî keep the glossary here.
- **Regeneration is cross-repo.** Editing the pipeline happens in SanskritLexicography; the
  regenerated data is copied here. Keep the two in sync on any schema change.
